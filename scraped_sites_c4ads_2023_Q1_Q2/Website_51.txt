Leopard skin seized from Odisha’s Kandhamal, 2 arrested




PTI 


Updated: April 24th, 2023, 11:55 IST


in
State, Top Stories 



 0








Share on FacebookShare on TwitterShare on WhatsAppShare on Linkedin









 

Kandhamal: Two persons were arrested with a leopard skin in Odisha’s Kandhamal district, police said Monday.
Acting on a tip-off, the Special Task Force of Odisha Police raided Khajuripada Sunday evening and arrested the two accused, they said.

Police said they were planning to sell the skin at Rs 5 lakh
