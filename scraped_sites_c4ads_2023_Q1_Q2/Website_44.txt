Image used for representational purpose DEHRADUN: The Uttarakhand Special Task Force (STF) arrested two men in possession of 4.7kg scales of Pangolin in Khatima town of US Nagar district on Saturday. According to STF, the two accused were identified as Asharam Prasad, 20, and Nanhe lal, 50, both residents of UP's Pilibhit.SP (STF) Ayush Agrawal said, "Based on a tip-off, we mobilised our officers who laid a dragnet to nab the two accused.On Saturday, we succeeded in arresting them near Puranpur road in Khatima with the Pangolin scales," said Agrawal. He further added, "During interrogation, the two confessed to hunting the wild animal in the jungles of Pilibhit in March. They were then taking its scale across the border to sell in Nepal." Agrawal also urged the people to inform the police against wildlife smuggling by contacting at 0135-2656202.
