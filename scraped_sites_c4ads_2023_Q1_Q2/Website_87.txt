On receiving the information, I, along with forest officers of the Dapoli and Chiplun forest range, laid a trap.” Bhate said at 4pm, two suspects were spotted. They were found carrying pangolin scales. The accused — Milind Vasant Sawant (55), from Malad, and Mina Mohan Kotia (62) from Lote village, were held.
