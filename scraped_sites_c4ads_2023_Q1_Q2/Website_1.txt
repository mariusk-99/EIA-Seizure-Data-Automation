    			         In Maharashtra, trio arrested for trading Pangolin scalesTNN | Jan 4, 2023, 10.02 AM ISTNAVI MUMBAI: Three persons have been arrested by the Mandangad unit of Ratnagiri police for illegally trading scales of the endangered Indian pangolin, while one accused escaped in a vehicle by taking advantage of the night darkness on the New Year's Day. Police have seized 4.372 kg of Indian pangolin scales from the nabbed accused. A manhunt has been launched for the absconding accused. The accused trio have been booked under illegal capturing the animal, hunting, etc sections of the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972 by the Mandangad police.
